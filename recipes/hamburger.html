Hamburger

Description

Imagine soft bread, golden brown eggs, and irresistible creaminess from grated cheese and toastinette that melt to perfection. Add a touch of freshness with crisp salad leaves, topped with ketchup and mayonnaise for a mouthwatering blend of flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 bread
  • 1 egg
  • 2 toastinettes cheese
  • ketchup
  • mayonnaise
  • 1 lettuce leaf

Steps

  1. Prepare an omelet, cook it, adding grated cheese (of your choice).
  2. Lightly toast your hamburger buns in a frying pan. Place a slice of cheese toastinette with mayonnaise on your first bun.
  3. Next, add your omelet, a little ketchup, a lettuce leaf, another slice of cheese, and your second piece of bread. And there you have it, all that's left to do is enjoy!
